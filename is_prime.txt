aval az karbar ye adad migirim.baad taqsime sahihe oon adad ro bar 2 toye ye 
moteqayere jadid be esme "a" zkhire mikonim.

dar edame agar adad bish az 2 bood, baqi mande oon adad ro bar adaade 2 ta "a"
barresi mikonim. agar baqi mande sefr shod, yani adad aval nist va "no" ro
print mikonim va az halqe kharej mishim.dar qeire in soorat "yes" ro print 
mikonim.

agar adad brabare 2 bood,"yes" ro print mikonim.choon 2 ye adade avale va 
algoritme bala baraye oon javab nemide.

agar adad 1 bood, 1 na avale, na morakab. pass "we have no answer to your input
" ro print mikonim.
